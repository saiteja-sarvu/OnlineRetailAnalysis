# Online Retail Analysis

Exploratory analysis and customer segmentation on the classic UCI "Online Retail" dataset — transactions from a UK-based online gift retailer between December 2010 and December 2011.

## Data

- `data/Online Retail.xlsx` / `data/Online_Retail.csv` — raw source data (541,909 rows: `InvoiceNo`, `StockCode`, `Description`, `Quantity`, `InvoiceDate`, `UnitPrice`, `CustomerID`, `Country`).
- `data/cleaned_online_retail.csv` — cleaned transaction-level data (cancellations, refunds, missing customers, and duplicates removed), generated by the notebook.
- `data/rfm_segments.csv` — one row per customer with Recency/Frequency/Monetary values, RFM scores, and segment label, generated by the notebook.

## Notebook (`notebooks/analysis.ipynb`)

1. **Load & clean** — drops rows with missing `CustomerID`, removes cancelled orders and non-positive quantity/price, drops duplicates.
2. **Descriptive analysis** — total revenue, average order value, monthly revenue trend, top products, top countries, top customers.
3. **RFM analysis** — builds Recency/Frequency/Monetary metrics per customer as of a snapshot date.
4. **RFM scoring & segmentation** — quartile-scores each customer on R/F/M and buckets them into segments (`Champions`, `Loyal Customers`, `Potential Loyalists`, `At Risk`, `Lost`).
5. **Pareto analysis** — checks what share of customers drive 80% of revenue.
6. **Cohort retention** — builds monthly acquisition cohorts and a retention-rate heatmap.
7. **Export** — writes the cleaned dataset and RFM segments to `data/` for downstream reporting.

## Dashboard

`dashboard/powerbi.pbix` — Power BI dashboard. Can be pointed at `data/cleaned_online_retail.csv` and `data/rfm_segments.csv` for a data source that mirrors the notebook's cleaning and segmentation logic.
